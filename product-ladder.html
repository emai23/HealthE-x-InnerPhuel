Key Takeaways:

  • Two Distinct Customer Bases: Corporate teams (B2B) and individual professionals (B2C) require completely different acquisition, messaging, and delivery models.
  • Pilot Scope: The Corporate Pilot only tests the B2B funnel (workshop + digital continuity). It does not require building the B2C ladder.
  • Future Optionality: Post-pilot, InnerPhuel can choose to build the B2C funnel (Path 1A or 1B) or continue scaling the B2B model exclusively (Path 2).
  • Pricing Ladder Context: The "Product Ladder" on page 6 of the Strategic Guide shows the full pricing/value range across both funnels—it's not a single customer journey.
